Artemis exerpt

The NASA Artemis missions represent humanity's ambitious return to the Moon, poised to expand our understanding of lunar exploration and pave the way for future crewed missions to Mars. Named after the twin sister of Apollo in Greek mythology, Artemis embodies NASA's commitment to advancing space exploration through sustainable lunar missions. These endeavors aim to establish a lunar outpost, conduct groundbreaking scientific research, and test new technologies crucial for deep space exploration. Artemis not only seeks to rekindle the spirit of lunar exploration but also to inspire new generations to reach for the stars and push the boundaries of human achievement once more.
